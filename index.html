About This Service

Trauma-Informed Care recognizes the widespread impact of trauma and prioritizes safety, trustworthiness, and healing. This approach uses specialized techniques like EMDR and somatic therapies to help process traumatic experiences safely.

Key Areas Addressed

EMDR Therapy Somatic Approaches Safety Planning Resilience Building

Benefits You Can Expect

  • Safe processing of traumatic memories
  • Reduction in trauma-related symptoms
  • Improved sense of safety and control
  • Enhanced emotional regulation
  • Stronger resilience and coping skills
  • Restored sense of empowerment

Who This Service Helps

  • Survivors of childhood trauma
  • Individuals with PTSD symptoms
  • Those who have experienced recent trauma
  • People with complex trauma histories
  • Anyone seeking trauma-informed support

The Process

  1. 1 Trauma assessment and safety planning
  2. 2 Stabilization and resource building
  3. 3 Gradual trauma processing using specialized techniques
  4. 4 Integration and consolidation of healing
  5. 5 Relapse prevention and ongoing support

What to Expect

Treatment begins with establishing safety and building coping resources before gradually processing traumatic memories using specialized, evidence-based techniques at your own pace.
